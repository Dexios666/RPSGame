ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a game between two people to resolve disputes. It involves using hand signals that represent rock, paper, and scissors. Rock beats scissors, paper beats rock, and scissors beat paper. If both players show the same signal, it's a tie, and the game continues until there is a winner.
